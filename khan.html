Coconut Roti

Ingredients:

Grated Coconut 2 cups
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Water As needed
Salt To taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix grated coconut, all-purpose flour, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into equal portions and shape them into flat rounds.
  4. Cook on a hot griddle until both sides are golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite curry or chutney.
